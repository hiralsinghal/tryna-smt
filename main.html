Happy Halloween!!

Fun facts

  • Halloween falls on 31st October.
  • Its origins date back to the ancient Celtic festival of Samhain.
  • People once carved turnips, not pumpkins.
  • Dressing up is an ancient tradition.
  • Trick-or-treating has medieval roots.
  • Halloween isn't the only spooky celebration.
  • A pumpkin is actually a fruit, not a vegetable.
